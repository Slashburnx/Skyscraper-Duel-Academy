Archetype Rulings

• Archetypal Cards
Cards that mention any archetype in their name or effect are archetypal.
• Non-Archetypal Cards
Cards that mention no archetype are non-archetypal. Example: Ash Blossom belongs to no archetype.
• Multi-Archetype Cards
Cards mentioning 2+ archetypes belong to each one they mention.
• Special Cases
Check the Notes column. Inclusions and exclusions listed there override standard rules.
• Refund Policy
Refund an archetype for half its original DP value. Use a Refund Ticket from the Shop.
Maximum 4 archetypes per duelist.
Each duelist holds up to 4 archetype slots simultaneously.
